About me

Software Engineer based out of Raleigh, NC

I was an operations manager at a top tier airline when I was introduced to software engineering. It took my breath away. I love video games and every aspect of information technology. When I found out about programming, it became my passion, my hobby, my new "video game", and my new job. I currently work with JavaScript, ReactJS, NodeJS, HTML, CSS, ExpressJS, MongoDB. I am a very motivated life-time learner, critical thinker, with extensive time-management skills. I am currently a FlatIron bootcamp student, while finishing my CS degree in Cybersecurity at Liberty University.
